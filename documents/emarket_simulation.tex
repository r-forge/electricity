\maketitle
\abstract{
The paper discusses game theoretic models for generation capacity investment decisions in a deregulated electricity market. The problem formulations involve open-loop, closed-loop and $S$-adapted Cournot equilibria. We give an example for the Austrian/German wholesale electricity market and analyze the welfare implications of these different strategic decision support models.
\\
\\
\textbf{Keywords:} Electricity markets, Investment decisions, Stochastic oligopoly model, Sample path optimization
\\
\\
\textbf{JEL Codes:} D43, L51
}
